General Data folder contains 6 types of Socio-Economic data. They are described as follows: 

1.   Monthly_US_Personal_Income.xls: Monthly US Personal Income in Billions of Dollars from 2016-01-01 to 2022-06-01. 
2.	Monthly_SNAP_Benefits Recipients._Number_AL.xlx: Monthly Number of SNAP Benefits Recipients in Alabama from 2000-01-01 to 2020-06-01.
3.   Weekly_US_GAS_price.xls: Weekly Gas Price in Dollars per Gallon from 2015-01-05 to 2022-08-22.
4.   Quarterly_Total_Personal_Income_in_AL.xls: Quarterly Total Personal Income in Alabama in Millions of Dollars from 2016-01-01 to 2022-01-01.
5.   Monthly_Unemployment_Rate_in_Lee_County_AL.xls: Monthly Unemployment Rate in Lee County, Alabama from 1990-01-01 to 2022-08-01.
6.   Monthly_US_Consumer_Price_Index.xls: Monthly US Consumer Price Index from 2016-01-01 to 2022-05-01.
